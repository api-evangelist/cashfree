EBITDA full form is Earnings Before Interest, Taxes, Depreciation, and Amortization, It is a financial metric that measures a company’s operating profitability. It shows how much profit a business generates from its core operations before accounting for financing costs, taxes, and non-cash expenses. Investors ask for profitability ratios.
