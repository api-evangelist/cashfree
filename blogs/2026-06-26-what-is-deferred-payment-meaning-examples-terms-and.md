Deferred payment refers to an agreement that allows customers to delay payment for goods or services until a specified future date, based on agreed payment terms. It improves cash flow for buyers, boosts conversions for businesses, and offers a flexible alternative to immediate payment. Shopping baskets get abandoned at the time of payment.
